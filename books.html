How and Why

Why | The Boats | Researching | More





A Flower in Winter was inspired by a deeply personal family story. David was moved by the experience of Jo Fitzgerald and her relative Sim Gokkes, a young Jewish composer who tragically disappeared during World War II. Jo’s enduring belief that her son had escaped — despite the silence that followed — planted the emotional seed for this story. A haunting stay aboard a dilapidated paddleboat further fuelled David’s imagination, sparking a journey into the shadows of history and memory.
