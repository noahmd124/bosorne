How Jo & I Met

Written by Jo Fitzgerald.


I first met David through a school mining project, where he kindly volunteered to give my class a talk about the mining in St Just and Pendeen. His passion and knowledge immediately stood out, and he offered to lead captivating workshops on World War I, a subject in which he is an expert. From there, he arranged music sessions for us at the local miners’ chapel, which became a place we visited often. We were always grateful for his time and generosity. Over time, we began writing to each other regularly, exchanging reflections and stories from David’s fascinating life. We affectionately called this ongoing correspondence “The Witterings,” and it was through these rich conversations that the inspiration for the book began to take shape.
