Judi123 - Amazon

I really enjoyed this book. The story is heart-wrenching, well researched and beautifully told and the writing draws you along, I was gripped from the first page. Set in the days leading up to the evacuation of Dunkirk (in which my own father was involved), it was totally believable. The humanity and bravery of the characters was well-drawn, especially the old Dutchmen and the young British officer. Can't recommend too highly!

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Jo Fitzgerald - Amazon

A Flower in Winter by David James is a gripping story following the escape of Sam, a young Jewish boy, from war torn Holland. It’s dramatic and tense description of the Nazi invasion make compelling reading - you feel like you are a member of the crew taking the journey with Sam. A master of detail, David James has obviously researched vastly to set the fictional story in a historically accurate setting. How refreshing to have a style of writing which draws you back into the era with such authenticity. Be prepared to get your tissues ready for parts of the ride. I can’t wait for the sequel!
